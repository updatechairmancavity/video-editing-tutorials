**Essential Editing Skills**

| Technique | Description | Shortcut (Premiere Pro) |
|-----------|-------------|-------------------------|
| Cutting | Split a clip | C (Razor tool) |
| Trimming | Adjust clip edges | N (Rolling Edit) |
| Moving | Drag clips on timeline | V (Selection tool) |
| Ripple Delete | Remove and close gap | Shift + Delete |
| Speed Control | Fast/slow motion | Right-click в†’ Speed |
| Reversing | Play backward | Speed в†’ Reverse |

**Cutting Techniques:**

```
Standard Cut:
1. Place playhead where you want to cut
2. Use Razor tool or press C
3. Click on clip to cut
4. Delete unwanted section
5. Close gap (Ripple Delete)

J-Cut:
1. Audio starts before video
2. Great for interviews
3. Creates natural transition
4. More professional feel

L-Cut:
1. Video starts before audio
2. Good for storytelling
3. Maintains flow
4. Common in dialogue
```

**Sequence Building:**

```
Step 1: Place main clips on timeline
Step 2: Arrange in logical order
Step 3: Trim to desired length
Step 4: Add transitions
Step 5: Adjust timing
Step 6: Preview and refine
Step 7: Add audio and effects
```

## вќ“ FAQ

<details>
<summary><b>What's the best video editing software for beginners?</b></summary>

CapCut is great for beginners, and DaVinci Resolve offers professional features with a free version.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>How long does it take to learn video editing?</b></summary>

3-6 months to become comfortable, 6-12 months for proficiency, 1-2 years for professional level.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>What are the most important video editing skills?</b></summary>

Cutting/trimming, audio editing, color correction, storytelling, and speed.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Can I edit video on a laptop?</b></summary>

Yes, modern laptops can handle video editing. More powerful laptops work better.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>What is the best format to export video?</b></summary>

H.264 or H.265 MP4 is the most universal format for web and social media.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>How do I remove background noise from audio?</b></summary>

Use noise reduction effects in your software or tools like Adobe Audition.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>What are LUTs?</b></summary>

LUTs (Look-Up Tables) are presets that transform the color of your video.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>What's the difference between color correction and grading?</b></summary>

Color correction fixes color issues. Color grading creates a creative look.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>How do I add subtitles?</b></summary>

Most software has caption tools. You can also use auto-caption features like CapCut's.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>Can I use Adobe Premiere Pro for free?</b></summary>

There's a free trial, but the full version requires a Creative Cloud subscription.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>What is the best software for social media videos?</b></summary>

CapCut is excellent for social media, especially TikTok and Instagram Reels.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>How do I stabilize shaky footage?</b></summary>

Use the Warp Stabilizer effect or stabilization tool in your editing software.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>What is a proxy file?</b></summary>

A low-resolution version of your footage that makes editing faster on slower computers.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>How do I become a professional video editor?</b></summary>

Learn the software, practice daily, build a portfolio, and start freelancing or applying for jobs.

</details>
